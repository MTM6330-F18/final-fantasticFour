Courses in the Interactive Media Design program range from 2 to 4 hours long. Students are required to also take 4 General Elective online courses throughout the two years. Courses may be offered in a classroom, entirely online or in hybrid modes which means it will be combined classroom sessions including online learning activities. Every full-time student will receive an Algonquin email which is used to communicate important information about the course events. Click here to view examples of course timetables.
